MTM Newcomers Resources Page

Help us reach newcomers! It will only take you a minute!

Now in its fifth year, MTM Newcomers is surveying individuals who have arrived in Canada within the last five years to collect data on their digital lives and access to technology, which will help inform resources and support tailored to their unique needs. As organizations working directly with newcomers, we invite you to share this information with them. Valid respondents will receive a $10 gift card for participating in the survey.


Share the link with them so they can register.
